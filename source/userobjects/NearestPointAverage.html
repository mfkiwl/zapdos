NearestPointAverage

Compute element variable averages for nearest-point based subdomains

The domain is virtually divided into a number of subdomains according to the nearest points provided by the users. For each provided point, a virtual subdomain is created around that point and consists of all regions of space that are closer to that point than any of the other provided points. Then, the variable average is computed over each individual subdomain separately.
